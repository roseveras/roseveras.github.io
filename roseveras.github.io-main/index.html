I'm Violet, a stop motion animator with a love for tactile storytelling — the kind you can hold in your hands before you put it on screen. I build my own puppets, fabricate sets, and frame every shot myself.

My debut film is an 8-minute stop motion work that explores how extreme escapism and obsessive fictional romance serve as a decaying coping mechanism against an isolating, bleak reality. It was shot entirely on DragonFrame and represents 2 years of work.

I'm currently looking to bring my skills into studio, commercial, and independent production work.

Film

Rotting Girl
Stop Motion · 8 min · 2026

Rotting Girl is a bleak psychological short film about a lonely woman who uses obsessive fictional romance to cope with an isolating, decaying world. It subverts traditional dystopian stories by focusing entirely on the dark reality of extreme escapism, showing a protagonist who actively chooses a fantasy world over facing her own reality.
